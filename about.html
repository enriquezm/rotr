Who am I?

I'm pretty bad at talking about myself, but here I go.

I'm a climber. Runner. Creator. Adventure seeker.

I've always been an introverted extrovert. I prefer spontaneity over routine. I prefer life to come as it does, and not plan too much for it. I guess I plan for the future while living in the moment, if that makes any sense.

I try to find what makes me come alive in life and go for it. The world needs more people to be alive and not just exist in it.

I've always been attracted to the dirty, rugged, character building outdoor lifestyle. I live for the days of true tests in the mountains, it's my serenity and my place of solitude. I enjoy photography, videography, and attempting to write. It's a way for me to be more mindful and see things from a different point of view. Good coffee is a must, and craft beer is a plus. I'm a sucker for good campfire singing, with beers all around. I play the native american flute. My partner in crime is Saige, and I've been in love with her for the past 10 years.

What is Rubber on the Rocks?

The name "Rubber on the Rocks" comes from my climbing days. Think climbing shoe rubber on a mountain face. It has since then evolved into just an outdoor journal I guess. This blog first started with paper and pencil about 2 years ago during my climbing adventures. And after some thinking and a build up of confidence, I figured I'd write my thoughts for you.
